HEARTshape - Pulse and Fitness

Unbad Apps

What does your pulse look like? Not just the number of beats it does per minute (i.e. the heart rate), but how does it behave? HEARTshape will let you see your heart in a whole new light!

HEARTshape measures your heart rate using your iPhone camera and flash. No need for a dedicated heart rate monitor!

HEARTshape gives you the possibility to see how blood flows through your finger with every heartbeat. How your heart rate changes - not minute by minute, but heartbeat by heartbeat. Changes to your heart rate will be displayed in an instant - with every new pulse!

HEARTshape will also show you how your heartbeats are distributed so that you can clearly see every time your heart skips a beat.

You should also take the opportunity to compare your heart rate to the general population. How does your resting heart rate measure up?

Features:
- Optical measurement of blood flow and detection of heartbeats using the built-in camera.
- Measurement of heart rate (beats per minute).
- Trend graph that shows how your heart rate changes with every heartbeat.
- Shows the variation of the beat to beat interval in realtime, i.e. the heart rate for each and every heartbeat.
- HealthKit integration to show you how your heart rate has changed over a longer period of time (enable in settings).

CardioBot - Heart Rate Tracker

Majid Jabrayilov

Your Apple Watch measures your heart rate every 4 minutes during the day. With CardioBot, you can easily understand the data captured by the Apple Watch so you can improve your lifestyle and discover notable patterns.

CardioBot is an iPhone app that reads heart rate data stored by the Apple Watch in HealthKit. The app separates readings in low, resting, high resting, and elevated heart rate through different colors and charts, allowing you to see averages at a glance.

CardioBot also supports viewing data captured during workouts, and it can also provide sleep analysis information for a complete dashboard of your heart rate. All of this is done with a minimal and elegant interface that makes it easy to view statistics for individual days, browse detailed timelines, and compare day-to-day changes.

This app requires an iPhone that has the Health App installed. Heart readings are read from the Health Data Store which is populated by your Apple Watch.

Featured by Apple in more than 85 countries!
